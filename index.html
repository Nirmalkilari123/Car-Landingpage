FRONX

Safety is a priority in the Suzuki Fronx with its Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) and Electronic Brake- force Distribution (EBD). ABS prevents wheel lock during braking, maintaining control. EBD balances brake force between front and rear wheels according to the vehicle's weight, enhancing stability.
